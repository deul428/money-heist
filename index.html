synopsis 1

recruit

The most perfect band of robbers in Spanish history is trying to steal 2.4 billion euros from the Royal Spanish Mint.

In order to carry out the biggest heist in history set in Madrid, a mysterious man known as the “Professor” recruits a group of people, who have a single odd characteristic: none of them has anything to lose. They choose city names such as Tokyo, Japan as their aliases.

synopsis 2

house

After taking 67 people hostage inside the Mint, the team plans to stay inside for 11 days and print money when they face elite police forces.

Stuck in Spain’s national coin and stamp factory, surrounded by police, dozens of hostages are in power. Whether their suicidal thoughts will lead to everything or nothing is anyone’s guess.
